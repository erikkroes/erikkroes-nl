!["Accessibility is on the agenda"  Red flag?](/assets/media/accessibility-is-on-the-agenda.png)

This makes me pay extra attention.

A statement like this gives the impression accessibility is a one time event:

* Something to be scheduled.
* Something "on the backlog".
* Something we'll do when we find the time (usually never).

Accessibility should be embedded. It is continuous.

Do not put it on your agenda. Put it in your Definition of Done!

Don't know where to start?
Try this methodology: https://www.solidstart.info/
Or read this explainer: https://inklusivo.nl/accessibility-fundamentals/
