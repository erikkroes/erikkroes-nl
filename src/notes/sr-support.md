What does it mean when people mention "limited screen reader support"?

It usually means something was built shoddy and doesn't follow specifications, and mostly people with screen readers are excluded by it. 

Screen reader support is about following specifications. And when you follow them, screen readers benefit. 

There is nothing in WCAG that says "support screen readers". It is about code quality and a lot of it can be caught by linters.

Screen readers don't need specific coding.
