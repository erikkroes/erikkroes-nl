If your text is white and light green, consider it unseen.

Don't expect people to read or notice what doesn't have contrast. (like this post)

WCAG 2.X AA says: 

* Large text (at least 19px and bold, or 24px) needs a contrast of 3∶1 or more. 
* Regular text needs a contrast of 4.5∶1 or more.
