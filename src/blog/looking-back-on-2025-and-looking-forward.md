It's 2026. Let's join the flood of retrospectives!

2025 was busy, very busy. I was doing very full weeks of freelance accessibility work. That means up to 3 days working for [VodafoneZiggo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VodafoneZiggo) combined with 2 days for the Dutch Design System: [NL Design System](https://nldesignsystem.nl/).
Combine this with short-term assignments, social media, blogging, training, presenting (including [NCDT](https://www.ncdt.nl/vorige-edities/2025/35-scaled-accessibility-how-big-dutch-companies-manage-accessibility/)), supporting the Dutch accessibility community through [IDEAA11Y](https://www.meetup.com/inclusive-design-accessibility/), running a Discord server, building for [inklusivo](https://inklusivo.nl/), administration, while trying to have a family life with 2 kids who are (at minimum) as bouncy as I am. Listing all this helps me reaffirm: it's been a busy year.
VodafoneZiggo is now down to 1 day a week. I finished working for NL Design System. My agenda feels entirely different now.
