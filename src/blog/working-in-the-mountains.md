Two weeks of Vorarlberg, Austria. Challenging climbs. Hiking along kilometers of mountain paths. Plenty of mountain sun. Drinking from streams. Too many schnitzels and fries. Amazing cable cars. More pictures than fit this post. Touching snow. Seeing glaciers. Exploring gorges. Holzkugelbahnen (IFYKYK?). Crossing ridges. Reaching peaks (!). A stay in a real mountain hut. And eating lots and lots of blueberries.

The list goes on and on. And now they miss the mountains even more than before. (Sorry not sorry. We all love the mountains 😬)
